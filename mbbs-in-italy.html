        About Italy

        • Italy, officially the Italian Republic, is located in Southern Europe. It shares borders with France, Switzerland, Austria, and Slovenia, and is surrounded by the Mediterranean Sea, giving it a unique cultural and historical heritage.

        • Italy is known for its rich cultural history, art, architecture, and contributions to science and medicine. The country offers a diverse environment, from the Alps in the north to the coastal regions and beautiful islands in the south.

        • Italy is considered one of the safest countries in Europe, with a high standard of living and a strong emphasis on education and healthcare, making it an attractive destination for international students.

        • Italy is a member of the European Union and the Schengen Area, allowing MBBS students to travel freely across Europe. It provides quality education, globally recognized degrees, and opportunities for further specialization within Europe.

        • The country boasts a lively cultural scene with world-famous cuisine, music, fashion, and festivals, offering students a rich and fulfilling experience outside the classroom.

        English Medium Instruction

        Globally Recognized Degrees

        Cultural & Academic Exposure

        Studying in Italy means living in a culturally rich environment with modern infrastructure, historical cities, and access to leading hospitals for clinical exposure.

        A. Apply for Codice Fiscale (Tax Code)

        Required For :

        • Opening a bank account
        • Signing a rental contract
        • Applying for a residence permit

        B. Permesso di Soggiorno (Residence Permit)

        Apply within 8 working days of arrival in Italy.

        Steps :

        • Collect the kit from your local Post Office
        • Fill and submit with documents + payment
        • Attend biometric appointment at the Questura

        C. University Physical Enrollment (if required)

        Some universities require in-person verification

        Visit with :

        • Original academic documents
        • Codice Fiscale
        • Residence permit receipt

        Complete enrollment & activate student credentials
